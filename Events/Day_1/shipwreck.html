SHIPWRECK

The participants are eminent celebrities who are on a sinking ship. They have to convinvce the captain(judge) to give them the last life jacket.

RULES

  • The participants will be given a character/role which they would have to enact and convince the judge to give them the last lifejacket
  • Vulgarity of any form will lead to direct disqualification
  • Time limit is 5 minutes.
  • You will be judged based on spontaneity, creativity and audience response.
