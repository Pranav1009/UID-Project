Channel Surfing

Judges have the remote which has control over the students on the stage, to enact the channels chosen by the judges

RULES

  • Number of participants per team : 5
  • Each institution may send only one team
  • The maximum time allowed per team is 3 minutes
  • Participants have to enact the channel given by the judge and change to different channels spontaneously
  • No vulgarity will be allowed
  • No preparation time will be given
  • Languages allowed are English, Hindi and Tamil
